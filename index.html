LAB OVERVIEW


The Computational Intelligence Laboratory (CI Lab.) at KAIST, Daejeon, Korea, is part of the Signal Processing Group at the School of Electrical Engineering. The mission of the CI Lab. is to perform innovative research work in the areas of image understanding and intelligent multimedia systems.

Members of the lab are investigating advanced technologies on a variety of multimedia system. Specifically, research has been focused on the followings.

: Image understanding, video analysis using deep learning, medical imaging, and nonlinear signal and image processing.
